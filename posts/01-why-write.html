why did i start writing?

2024-10-26

This post is mostly written for myself.

I expect most posts here will be for myself. It's not that I don't care about other people, but I don't want to cater to some imaginary reader persona. If I did, I think that nothing I produced would be worth reading. My goal here is to just explore writing and let it happen one post at a time.

I've thought a long time about starting to share my thoughts but somehow never got around to it. I honestly don't even have a good reason for wanting to write. It just appeared out of nowhere. It's always been difficult for me to figure out what I want to pursue in any given moment. Maybe part of me hopes that writing will help me better understand myself.
